# Morning routine tracker

## Overview
A mobile app that helps users create, customize, and maintain consistent morning routines through guided setup, daily tracking, and progress insights. The app focuses on building sustainable habits by breaking down morning routines into manageable steps and providing motivation through streaks and achievements.

## Problem
People know morning routines are beneficial but lack structure, accountability, and tracking to build and maintain them consistently over time

## Target User
Adults aged 25-45 who want to improve their productivity and well-being by establishing better morning habits but struggle with consistency

## Features
- Customizable routine builder with pre-built templates
- Daily check-off tracking with time estimates
- Streak counters and progress visualization
- Smart notifications and wake-up reminders
- Habit analytics and weekly/monthly reports
